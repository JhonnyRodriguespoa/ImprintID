# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and inclusive environment.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful
- collaborate constructively
- welcome diverse perspectives
- avoid harassment or discrimination

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment
- abusive language
- personal attacks
- intimidation

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove contributions or participants that violate this code.
